About PageWhisper

PageWhisper is an advanced AI-powered platform that converts "handwritten or printed PDFs" into editable and downloadable text. Using Google Vision API and deep learning techniques, our platform ensures high accuracy and efficiency in text recognition.

Designed for **students, researchers, and professionals, PageWhisper streamlines the process of text extraction, making it effortless to digitize documents with just a few clicks.

AI-Powered OCR

Using Google's Vision API, PageWhisper provides state-of-the-art optical character recognition.

Seamless Upload & Download

Upload PDFs, extract text, and download results in various formats like DOCX and PDF.

Secure & Private

All files are automatically deleted after processing, ensuring data security**.

Access your conversions from any device, anytime, with a user-friendly interface.
